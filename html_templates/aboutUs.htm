GOD’S LOVE FOUNDATION (GLOVE FOUNDATION)

GOD’S LOVE FOUNDATION (GLOVE FOUNDATION) is a not-for-profit organization that is established with the aim of promoting preventive health in Ghana. The main objective of its establishment is to promote the dissemination and exchange of medical and scientific knowledge among students, Health service workers and the general public at large. It started softly as a students’ club titled CITA-KNUST (2010) where it was only operational in the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology. After a year, the then club became GLOVE FOUNDATION-GHANA in other to spread its selfless goals to all angles of the country.

The foundation has been successful in organizing and supporting important health programs in Ghana. So far, our service has reached over 15,000 people living in Ghana and still counting. Our focus is not only on the dissemination and exchange of medical knowledge among health service workers and the general public but also build facilities such as hospitals, fitness centers, recreation centers and other facilities to aid the fight against preventable diseases. The foundation is made up of students’ organizations in health, private health institutions and individuals who endorse our goal. Because of our broad vision, we still welcome everyone to come on board so we can combat diseases and sicknesses.
